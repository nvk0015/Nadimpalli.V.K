Personal Statement

Having grown up in an agricultural environment, I was fascinated by the impact of machine systems on modifying and optimizing manual and human-intensive processes. My curiosity for technology, automation, and customization influenced my desire to explore the depths of Artificial Intelligence and its ability to transform the fields of automation systems and product development.

About Me

From the busy streets to the research labs of Germany, my journey in engineering, science, and technology has been a piece of evidence of my passion for learning, my adaptability to different environments, and my constant search for innovation. My formative years were filled with a mixture of academic rigor and extracurricular activities. Balancing strict school hours with field hockey and cricket games provided me with a strong work ethic and the ability to multi-task effectively. My curiosity was further ignited in 11th grade when I was introduced to the fascinating world of statistics and probability. The concepts of probability distributions and decision-making under uncertainty captivated me, and I began to apply these principles to various aspects of my life. When visiting my family's rice farm, I experienced first-hand how machines can simplify labor-intensive tasks. This exposure to engineering in action reinforced my admiration for the field and my desire to contribute to its advancement. It was this mix of academic curiosity and practical experience that led me to choose mechanical engineering in my bachelor's degree.

My Bachelor's degree opened my eyes to the vast field of automation technologies, especially the use of programming to redesign and optimize manufacturing processes. I delved into the intricacies of computer-aided manufacturing (CAM), where I was introduced to game-changing concepts such as machine vision, robotics, and artificial intelligence (AI). These cutting-edge technologies fascinated me with their potential to revolutionize the industry and increase human productivity.

I became more fascinated with applied AI during my Master's degree at Rosenheim University of Applied Sciences in Germany. The opportunity to work with industry leaders such as Krones AG provided me with invaluable hands-on experience in developing and implementing AI-based solutions for real-world applications. This experience has ignited my enthusiasm for applying AI to automate tasks, personalize user experiences, and optimize processes.

My doctoral studies at OTH Regensburg enabled me to delve even deeper into the theory of AI and its applications. I am currently researching the AI-based personalization of learning management systems. As I move forward, I want to stay at the cutting edge of innovation and push the boundaries of what is possible. I want to contribute my expertise to the development of AI-driven solutions that can solve real-world problems and improve lives. My goal is to become a leading researcher and innovator in the field of applied AI and leave a long-lasting transformative impact.
